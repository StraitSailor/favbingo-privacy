Privacy Policy — Fav Bingo

Last updated: 18 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Fav Bingo (package com.favbingo.app, the “App”) handles information. Fav Bingo is an offline bingo caller and card tracker. We respect your privacy: the App does not create accounts, and does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to us or any server.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Fav Bingo has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. We do not gather your name, email, location, contacts, or device identifiers.

Device Permissions and How They Are Used

  • Camera — used only when you turn on the optional “Vision Tracker” to recognise printed bingo numbers. Image processing happens on your device using on-device text recognition. Camera images are not saved, uploaded, or shared.
  • Microphone — used only when you turn on the optional “Voice Tracker” to recognise spoken numbers. Speech is processed by your device’s built-in speech-recognition service to detect numbers. We do not record, store, or transmit audio. Speech recognition is provided by your device/operating system and is subject to your device provider’s privacy terms.
  • Internet / Network state — declared by the App and its components. The App’s gameplay works offline; we do not send personal data over the network.

Data Stored on Your Device

Bingo sets you choose to save (card layouts, page names) and your in-app settings are stored only on your device’s local storage. This data never leaves your device and is removed if you delete the App or its data.

Children

Fav Bingo is a general-audience bingo game. It contains no real-money gambling, betting, or prizes. It does not knowingly collect information from children.

Third Parties

We do not sell or share data with third parties. The App uses on-device libraries (e.g. for text recognition) that process data locally and do not transmit it to us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
